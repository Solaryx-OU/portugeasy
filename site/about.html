Our Story & Mission

PortugEasy was created to make high-quality Portuguese education accessible worldwide through human-centered teaching and an academically rigorous curriculum.

Mission

Empower learners to thrive in Portuguese-speaking environments through evidence-based learning, cultural immersion, and lifelong community support.

Born from Academic Excellence

PortugEasy began as a collaboration between experienced online educators and Professor Ana Santos of Nova University Lisbon. Together we crafted a curriculum that bridges academic rigor with the flexibility of digital learning.

2015 — The Idea

Our founders met at a Lisbon language conference and envisioned an online school that delivers Nova-quality teaching to busy professionals.

2017 — First Cohorts

With a pilot grant from Nova University, we launched the first A1–B2 cohorts with learners across Europe, Africa, and the Americas.

2023 — Global Community

Today we support thousands of learners with live classes, coaching, and community events spanning 12 time zones.

Teaching Philosophy

Every course balances communicative practice, cultural exploration, and structured grammar so you can apply Portuguese with confidence in academic, professional, and social settings.

Communicative First

Live conversation labs and breakout sessions build fluency while reinforcing grammar in context.

Data-Informed Coaching

Progress dashboards and formative assessments highlight strengths and guide targeted feedback.

Cultural Immersion

Weekly culture capsules spotlight Lusophone music, media, and guest speakers to deepen connection.

Community Impact

Scholarships
