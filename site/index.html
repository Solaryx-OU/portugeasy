Curriculum Designed with Nova University

Our lead curriculum architect is an associate professor at Nova University Lisbon who ensures every module reflects current research in language acquisition and Lusophone culture.

Research-Based Learning

Gain fluency through scaffolded lessons, authentic materials, and task-based learning activities built on Nova University methodologies.

Real-World Application

Practice Portuguese through cultural immersion workshops, conversation clubs, and partner internships with Lusophone organizations.
